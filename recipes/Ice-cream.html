Vanilla and Chocolate Ice Cream

Description

This recipe provides a step-by-step guide on how to make creamy and rich vanilla and chocolate ice cream from scratch. Using fresh ingredients and simple techniques, you can create your own delectable ice cream at home to satisfy your sweet tooth cravings!

This recipe yields 4 servings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of whole milk
  • 3/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of pure vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons of unsweetened cocoa powder (for chocolate ice cream)
  • 1/2 cup of chocolate chips (optional)
  • Pinch of salt

Steps for Cooking:

  1. Prepare the ice cream base:
    • In a mixing bowl, combine the heavy cream, whole milk, sugar, and salt. Whisk until the sugar is fully dissolved.
    • Divide the mixture in half. To one half, add the vanilla extract and stir well to create the vanilla base. For the chocolate base, mix in the cocoa powder until fully dissolved.
  2. Chill the mixture:
    • Refrigerate both mixtures for at least 2 hours or until very cold.
  3. Churn the ice cream:
    • Pour the vanilla base into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions. Repeat for the chocolate base.
  4. Freeze the ice cream:
    • Transfer the churned ice cream into airtight containers. For chocolate ice cream, stir in the chocolate chips if desired. Freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm.
  5. Serve:
    • Scoop the ice cream into bowls and serve immediately. Enjoy!

Enjoy your homemade Vanilla and Chocolate Ice Cream!
