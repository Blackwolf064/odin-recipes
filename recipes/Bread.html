Fluffy Soft Homemade Bread

Description

This recipe for fluffy soft homemade bread is perfect for beginners and seasoned bakers alike. It's easy to follow and yields a loaf that's soft, airy, and delicious. Ideal for sandwiches, toast, or as a side with your favorite meal.

This recipe makes one large loaf.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (2 and 1/4 tsp) of active dry yeast
  • 2 tbsp of granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 cup of warm water (110째F/45째C)
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 2 tbsp of unsalted butter (softened)
  • 1 egg (room temperature)
  • 1 tbsp of oil for greasing the bowl

Steps for Baking:

  1. Activate the Yeast:
    • In a small bowl, mix the warm water, sugar, and yeast together. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy. This means the yeast is activated.
  2. Prepare the Dough:
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, salt, milk, butter, and the frothy yeast mixture.
    • Mix until the ingredients are well combined and a sticky dough forms.
    • Add the egg and continue mixing. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 8-10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  3. First Proof:
    • Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover with a clean kitchen towel. Let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours, or until it has doubled in size.
  4. Shape the Dough:
    • Punch down the dough to release air bubbles. Turn it onto a floured surface and shape it into a loaf.
    • Place the shaped dough into a greased loaf pan.
  5. Second Proof:
    • Cover the loaf pan with a kitchen towel and let it rise again for about 30-45 minutes, or until the dough reaches the edge of the pan.
  6. Bake the Bread:
    • Preheat your oven to 350째F (175째C). Bake the bread for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the bread sounds hollow when tapped.
  7. Cool and Serve:
    • Remove the bread from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 5-10 minutes. Then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.
    • Slice and enjoy your fluffy, soft homemade bread warm or at room temperature.

Enjoy your fluffy soft homemade bread fresh out of the oven with butter or your favorite spread!
